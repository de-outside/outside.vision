Hi,


after a longer hiatus I am sending a new mix which I recorded on 17.10.2024 for Shella Radio. I loosely follow up on my recent mixes, musical experiences and other thought processes.

 

I enjoy exploring the perception of music, its boundaries and exploring where things can be pushed. Therefore I approached the mix a little more conceptually and turned the usual intro-climax-outro flow upside down. This resulted in a progression that I would describe as teaser-tension-climax breaking away from the expected flow of the mix and thereby creating a certain tension in the listeners.

 

You can play the recording with the video on YouTube, the audio itself should be available on SoundCloud soon.


Tune in, enjoy & don't forget to let me know

Dominik
