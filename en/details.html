OUTSIDE.VISION

I created Outside (aka outside.vision) as a personal medium at the intersection of communicator, social network, blog or whiteboard. The idea for something like this came after a long time since I left most social networks. I began to struggle with the absence of a medium through which I could communicate my projects and thoughts to those around me and at the same time have a creative dialogue with friends.
